01 · The work

A brand film for a third-generation florist — craftsmanship as the whole story.

Kremp Florist in Willow Grove, Pennsylvania, is a three-generation family business. I directed and shot a cinematic brand-story film centered on what the company actually does well — design, arrangement, the unglamorous early-morning logistics of getting flowers in front of people on the right day. We followed designers from cooler to bench, watched arrangements come together from raw stems, and let the craft carry the narrative.

The film was built as a hero piece for the website and as something the family could send to retail partners and wholesale buyers. There's no voiceover and almost no on-screen copy — the entire pitch is the work itself. For a brand that's been around for decades, the right play was to show the room, the hands, and the flowers, and trust the audience.
